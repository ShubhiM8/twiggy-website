  • Shop normally—Twiggy helps you compare with a score + confidence.
  • Choose better by picking an alternative that matches your budget and values.

What the Twiggy Score means

Honest + trustworthy language

Twiggy Score (0–100) is a best-effort estimate designed to help you compare products quickly. It uses available product and brand signals and shows a confidence level because coverage varies by product. When possible, Twiggy also provides a “why this score” explanation.

  • Higher score = more sustainable signals (based on available data)
  • Confidence helps you understand how complete the data is
  • Scores improve over time as coverage expands
